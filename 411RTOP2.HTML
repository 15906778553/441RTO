school history

The business school was established in the 1960s and was initially a small research institution aimed at cultivating the abilities of future business leaders. In the following decades, the school continued to expand and became one of the most famous business schools in Australia. Schools are not only committed to providing high-quality education, but also play an important role in the business sector, making outstanding contributions to the community. Today, the school has modern facilities and a professional teaching staff, providing students with countless opportunities to realize their career dreams and achieve success in the business field.
